Why Conserve Indian Cows?

Indian cows play a crucial role in sustainable farming, Ayurveda, and environmental balance. Due to urbanization and lack of awareness, native breeds are declining. Our mission is to educate, support, and protect these valuable animals.

Our Mission

- Promoting organic farming using cow-based products.
- Improve breeding programs using AI recommendations.
- Support farmers through education and digital tools.
